Holiday Spend Tracker - Getting Started

Here’s a gentle, step‑by‑step guide so you can be managing your holiday budget like a pro in just a few minutes.

Setup in 3 steps

Step 1

Set the budget

  1. Enter your holiday spending budget where prompted.

Step 2

Enter the dates of your holiday

  1. Click in the start date box and select the start date of your holiday in the calendar.
  2. Click in the end date box and select the end date of your holiday in the calendar.
  3. Click Calculate Budget.
  4. The app will now provide a suggested average daily budget and unlock the ability to add expenses.

Step 3

Enter Expenses

  1. Enter the expense desciption (optional)
  2. Enter the value of the expense
  3. Click Add
  4. As you add expenses the overall balance will be adjusted to show what money remains of your budget.

Useful info

  • All changes saved locally
  • No account required
  • Works in any web browser

What you need

  • Webrowser - e.g. Chrome, IE, Safari
  • Your Holiday Spending Budget
  • The Dates of your Trip
