Travel Spend Tracker FAQs

Got questions about using the Travel Spend Tracker? Our FAQ provides the answers to help easily manage your daily spending and control travel money stress-free.

If your question isn't covered in the list, then simply ask it via the form at the bottom of the page and we will get back to you with the answer.

How do I reset my data?

To completely reset your data and start a new budget, click start new at the top of the page.

Can I export my data?

No, you cannot currently export your data.

How do I edit an expense item?

To edit an expense item, simply delete the item and then enter the corrected data.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, the app works offline on your device where you setup the budget.

Can I access the budget on multiple devices?

No, you can only access your current budget on the device and browser you started the budget on.

Can I create multiple budgets to track?

No, you can only create one budget at the time.
