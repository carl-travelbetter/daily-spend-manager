Holiday Spend Tracker - Adjust Settings

Learn how to easily adjust the settings of the Holiday Spend Tracker.

Stress Free Holiday Expense Management

How to adjust your budget

  1. Click Adjust settings in the expenses area.
  2. The app will now present the original settings area with the budget and duration fields.
  3. Enter a new figure for the budget and click Calculate
  4. If you want help with this, check out the getting started guide.

How to adjust your trip duration

  1. Click Adjust settings in the expenses area.
  2. The app will now present the original settings area with the budget and duration fields.
  3. Enter a new figure for the duration and click Calculate
  4. If you want help with this, check out the getting started guide.

How to start a new budget

  1. Click Start New.
  2. The app will now clear all the data and allow you to start a new trip beginning with entering the budget and duration.
