Getting Started

Here’s a gentle, step‑by‑step setup so you can be ready in just a few minutes.

Keep things simple—follow the TL;DR if you’re in a hurry, or use the detailed steps if you want more context.

TL;DR

1. Start New → Set a budget → Select Your Trip Dates → Click Calculate.

2. Add expenses as you go.

3. Monitor your balance.

Useful info

  • All changes saved locally
  • No account required
  • Works in any web browser

What you need

  • Webrowser - e.g. Chrome, IE, Safari
  • Your Holiday Spending Budget
  • The Dates of your Trip

Setup in 4 steps

Step 1

Set the budget

Enter your holiday spending budget where prompted.

Step 2

Enter the dates of your holiday

Click in the end date box and select the last day of your holiday in the calendar.

-- The app will now provide a suggested average daily budget and unlock the ability to add expenses. --

Step 3

Enter Expenses

Enter the expense desciption (optional)

Enter the value of the expense

Click Add

-- As you add expenses the overall balance will be adjusted to show what money remains of your budget. --
