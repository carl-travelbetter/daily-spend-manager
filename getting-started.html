Getting Started with the Travelbetter Daily Spend App

tl;dr

Start New - Set a budget - Set an End Date - Add Expenses as you Go throughout your holiday

Useful info

  • All changes saved locally
  • No account required
  • Works in any web browser

What you need

  • Webrowser - e.g. Chrome, IE, Safari
  • Your Holiday Spending Budget
  • The End Date of your Trip

Detailed Getting Started Guide

1. Set a budget

Enter your holiday spending budget where prompted.

2. Select the end date of your holiday

Click in the end date box and select the last day of your holiday in the calendar.

-- The app will now provide a suggested average daily budget and unlock the ability to add expenses. --

3. Enter Expenses

Click in the end data box and select the last day of your holiday.

-- As you add expenses the overall balance will be adjusted to show what remains of your budget. --
