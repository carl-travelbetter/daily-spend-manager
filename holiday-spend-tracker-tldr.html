Holiday Spend Tracker TL:DR

Short on time but looking for an easy way to manage your holiday money? This TL;DR guide will have you up and running with the Travel Spend Tracker in less than 2 minutes.

TL;DR

  1. Click Start New → Enter a budget → Enter your holiday duration in days → Click Calculate.
  2. Add expenses as you go through your holiday.
  3. Monitor your balance to ensure you don't run out of money.

What you need to get started

  • Webrowser - e.g. Chrome, IE, Safari
  • Your Holiday Spending Budget
  • The Dates of your Trip

Useful info

  • All changes saved locally
  • No account required
  • Works in any web browser
